“No carry-on fees” simply means everyone pays carry-on fees

I can’t imagine why Congress is involved in this, but apparently it’s an outrage that airlines are charging fees for carry-on bags. The fallacy is that by banning such fees, the consumer is saved money. In fact, it’s the opposite. Since all passengers will be allowed to carry-on without a separate fee, that fee will simply be built into every ticket, regardless of whether the traveler actually takes a carry-on.
