Educator extension for BlogEngine.net released

Published by Matt Sherman · May 19, 2009

I’ve created an extension for BlogEngine.Net to improve typography. It “educates” the text, so that quotes and apostrophes are transformed into their smart (curly) counterparts. This blog uses it.

It’s called Educator and details are here.
