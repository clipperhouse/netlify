What’s this?

This is a pet project by Matt Sherman, to provide a simple, distraction-free way of listening to my favorite classical stations. Maybe they’re your favorites too.

I’m partial to terrestrial, local radio with real DJs telling you about the weather.

It doesn’t have my favorite station.

Let me know! mwsherman@gmail.com or @clipperhouse.
