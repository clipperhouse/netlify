Your site doesnt need a novel progress indicator

Theres been healthy talk about progress indicators in the age of ajax. In our will-to-design I think some things are being missed.

First, your browsers UI already includes a progress indicator, and perhaps more than one:

Your user is accustomed to these things, not unlike the back button. Why would we introduce a novel UI element, adding to the users cognitive load, when we already have them?

The shortcoming here is that, perhaps, the browser doesnt offer obvious programmatic access to the above, or that it doesnt occur to us to use it.

So I published this hack to put the browser in a loading state programmatically. I am sure it can be improved upon, let me know your thoughts.
