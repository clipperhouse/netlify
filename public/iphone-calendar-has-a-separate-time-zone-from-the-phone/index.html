The iPhone calendar has a separate time zone from the phone

After my recent move to NYC, I discovered that my Google Calendar was 3 hours off from my iPhone calendar. No worries, just go into the iPhone SettingsGeneralDate & Time and make sure the phone understands the time zone. Huh, well, its set automatically, over the air.

There is a setting in Google Calendar for time zone. Changed that. No joy.

Finally, I looked deep into the iPhones Mail, Contacts and Calendar settingsand way at the bottom of that screen, you set the time zone for the calendar. And it wasnt the same as the phones time zone. Sheesh. Now my calendars are synced up.
